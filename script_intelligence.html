Script Intelligence

The next evolution in script analysis powered by our structured data and AI

Beyond Basic Script Parsing

Talking with AI can be helpful, but AI models easily get lost when dealing with a complete script. Without proper structure and context, AI struggles to provide precise references and consistent analysis.

Our AI-Powered Script Parsing pipeline gives a powerful foundation for AI models to analyze scripts. This allows AIs to combine their reading of the script with data analysis they run on structured data. The result: responses that are grounded, extensive, and provide citations that enhance trust.

Script Intelligence transforms how AI understands scripts by:

  • Creating structured data that AI can efficiently analyze
  • Enabling precise scene and character references
  • Facilitating detailed VFX shot identification and classification
  • Supporting comprehensive script analysis for bidding preparation
  • Providing a foundation for advanced metrics and visualizations

The Power of Structured Script Data

Here's a concrete example based on our demo analysis of No Country for Old Men. We asked a question that requires deep script understanding: identifying every scene where two specific characters appear together.

The Challenge:

List the exact scenes where Moss and Carla Jean appear together, with precise scene references.

Standard AI with PDF Only

Basic Results

When given only a PDF of the script, the AI:

  • ✅ Identified some scene locations correctly
  • ❌ Could not provide precise scene numbers
  • ❌ Missed the critical phone call scene
  • ❌ Provided limited dialogue context
  • ❌ Offered vague page references
AI RESPONSE

Trailways Bus - Day • Moss and Carla Jean are on the bus • He explains he's going to Del Rio to borrow a car from Eldon • Carla Jean expresses concern: "I got a bad feelin', Llewelyn"

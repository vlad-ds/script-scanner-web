About Script Scanner Pro

Learn about our story and the technology behind this service

Our Story

Script Scanner Pro is an independent project developed by Vlad Gheorghe, in close collaboration with VFX industry advisors. As a data engineer with experience at large companies, I recognized that my technical skills could help solve a common problem in the VFX industry: the tedious and time-consuming task of manually parsing scripts for VFX bidding.

Through partnerships with industry professionals who shared their challenges, I developed this tool to save VFX producers, supervisors and coordinators countless hours spent generating manual breakdowns, while improving accuracy and consistency across their bidding workflows.

This is a fully independent project, not affiliated with any VFX studio or production company, ensuring complete confidentiality and neutrality.

Mission

We're on a mission to streamline the VFX bidding process by automating the most time-consuming aspects of script breakdown, allowing creative professionals to focus their energy on what matters most: creating stunning visual effects.

By eliminating the manual tedium from script parsing, our goal is to help VFX companies of all sizes respond to bid requests more quickly, accurately, and competitively.

The Technology

Intelligent Script Processing

Script Scanner Pro leverages advanced technologies to understand screenplay formats and extract meaningful data. The solution recognizes various script elements including:

Accurate OCR on Raw PDFs

The system extracts text from PDFs with high accuracy, handling various screenplay formats and preserving the critical formatting that indicates different script elements.

Scene Detection

The system identifies scene headings, tracks scene numbers, and detects scene transitions, ensuring accurate segmentation of the script.

Action & Dialogue Separation

The system accurately classifies text as either action descriptions or dialogue, crucial for understanding potential VFX requirements.

VFX Context Understanding

The system identifies language that suggests VFX requirements, helping to flag potential shot considerations.

Multilingual Capabilities

Script Scanner Pro can process scripts in multiple languages and provide English translations when needed, making it ideal for international productions.

Ongoing Enhancements

We continuously improve the system based on user feedback and new script formats, ensuring increasingly accurate results over time.

Full PDF Parsing

Our advanced technology extracts all content from your script PDFs with high accuracy, preserving the structure and formatting crucial for screenplay understanding.

Content Classification

Data Protection & Security

Your scripts contain sensitive creative and intellectual property. We take their protection seriously.

Secure Storage

All script files are stored in encrypted storage with limited access. We maintain strict access controls and implement industry-standard security practices to protect your content.

Legal Protection

We happily sign your NDAs before processing any scripts. Our commitment to confidentiality ensures your creative work remains protected throughout the entire process.

No Model Training

Your scripts are never used to train our AI models. We process your content only for the purpose of delivering your requested service and do not retain or repurpose your data.

Data Retention Policy

We maintain your data only for the duration required to complete your service. Upon request, we can delete all your script files after delivery or implement custom retention policies for your project.

Independent Project

This is a fully independent project, not affiliated with any VFX studio or production company, ensuring complete confidentiality and neutrality when handling your script data.

AI Accuracy Guarantee

Our AI system is continuously improving to provide the highest level of accuracy. If you find any parsing errors or issues, we'll fix them at no additional cost.
